# **App Name**: TechTier Talent

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Resume Interface: A responsive, single-page application showcasing three years of desktop support achievements, certifications, and career growth.
- AI Professional Tailor: An intelligent tool that takes a job description and suggests refinements to your personal statement to highlight matching IT support skills.
- Technical Skills Matrix: An interactive dashboard categorized by Hardware, OS (Windows/macOS), and Networking capabilities for quick technical assessment.
- Problem-Solving Gallery: A visual showcase for documenting specific high-impact resolutions and complex hardware deployments completed during professional tenure.
- Integrated Lead Capture: A streamlined contact mechanism for recruiters and hiring managers to request more details or schedule an interview.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Electric Slate (#3E54D3), reflecting stability and technical precision.
- Background: Midnight Obsidian (#16181D), chosen for a premium, agency-style dark mode that minimizes eye strain.
- Accent: Arctic Glaze (#72CCE0), used for highlighting technical proficiency levels and key interaction points.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' for headlines to provide a computerized, tech-forward aesthetic, and 'Inter' for body text for maximum legibility of professional history.
- Monochromatic line icons with subtle glows to represent different hardware components and networking protocols.
- Asymmetrical grid layout that prioritizes a vertical 'event-based' timeline for experience, typical of professional technical portfolios.
- Micro-transitions on scroll for skill bars and entrance fades for portfolio cards to provide a polished, high-end feel.
